I
About Félix

A boutique advisory practice rooted in Puerto Rico.

FS
Félix Silvestrini

Félix Silvestrini operates a strategic real estate advisory practice serving investors, developers, and infrastructure stakeholders across Puerto Rico — with particular depth in workforce housing coordination for organizations bringing operations to the island.

The practice is built around a single conviction: in Puerto Rico, market knowledge is local and relationships matter. Successful execution requires an advisor who is on the island, fluent in both markets and languages, and connected to an active network of agents, landlords, and operators across every municipality where projects unfold.

Félix is licensed in Puerto Rico (Lic. C-23200) and affiliated with Keller Williams Puerto Rico — bringing the infrastructure of an international brokerage to a focused, advisor-first engagement model.

Renewable Energy · Utility-Scale Solar Construction
